Our History

Adagio is an award-winning wine company from Spain with vineyards located all around the world. Our Spanish tempranillo delivers contrasting flavors of leather and cherries, and is balanced with earth and fruit flavors, which makes it the perfect choice to share with friends.


Our company has humble beginnings, starting from selling wine locally to now selling wine worldwide. We originally started as a family-run team that slowly grew into a team with ten employees, and now we have over 70 employees employed across all vineyards and factories.
